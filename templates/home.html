ImgQuik

Quick & easy placeholder images

Easy

Creating an image on the fly is as easy as a pie with ImgQuik.

Simply provide a url in the src attribute of an img tag in your HTML file as follows:

<img src="imgquik.herokuapp.com/100x80" />

Flexible

ImgQuik provides room for customization of your image dimensions as well as color.

<img src="imgquik.herokuapp.com/150x400/CCC/000" />

Fast

Generating images are fast since we make efficient use of your browser's cache.
